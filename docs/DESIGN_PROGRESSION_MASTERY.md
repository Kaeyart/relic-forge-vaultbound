# Design Lock — Progression and Mastery

The game should be playable by newcomers and abusable by veterans.

## New players

A new player should be able to pick skills that look cool, equip obvious upgrades, and clear early content without reading a wiki.

## Veteran players

A veteran player should discover hidden power through:

- affix pool knowledge;
- forge potential planning;
- targeted salvaging;
- skill gem and support gem interactions;
- unique item archetypes;
- risky restoration routes;
- activity-specific farming.

## Balance philosophy

Fun and power fantasy matter more than sterile balance. If a build takes effort and knowledge to assemble, it is allowed to become absurd.
